Current challenges in payment





In the large space of digital payments we narrowed down to consumers and merchants. During our discovery phase, we explored a wide range of topics related to authentication in digital payments. Some topics we explored laterally in this space:

- Payments in a social context;
- Merchant inertia, and getting merchants to adopt new technology;
- Using multiple identities in different contexts;
- How consumers feel about new authentication methods,

And a new authentication paradigm that ties these all together called Continuous Authentication




Solve these challenges with
Continuous Authentication

With Continuous Authentication →

Continuous Authentication is an idea that has been bouncing around the digital payments space for the past few years but will little implementation to show for it. With Continuous Authentication we can eliminate a lot of the waiting and touchpoints in this diagram above, showing checkout sequences in both physical stores and online shops. Continuous Authentication removes the hassle of juggling multiple cards and passwords, as well as reducing false declines.




Proof of Concept: A Face
Recognition Demo


We developed a proof of concept for a facial recognition authentication experience. This is a slow burn project replicating some of the features of Project Abacus’ demo in the studio that shows a certainty rating for a user in the camera.
